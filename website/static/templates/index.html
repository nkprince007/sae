SAE


SAE India collegiate chapter of NIT Durgapur was conceived in 2007 with an idea to integrate the students of the college into international fabric of automobile engineering. Presently 200 students are enjoying the benefits of SAE India membership.

We organize a number of innovative events, workshops and lectures round the year and a competitive place where the sharpest minds battle in the quest to achieve disctinction. The amalgamation of all these events under a single roof, during the month of February finally results in the unique odyssey called Motor Zundung which is the largest Auto fest of Eastern India. SAE NIT Durgapur also publishes its annual magazine called Autopia.

SAE BAJA


NDORS (NIT Durgapur Off-Road Sports), is the official BAJA team of NIT Durgapur. We design and fabricate a BAJA buggy every year to participate in BAJA SAE INDIA.

The students of the college themselves organize workshops about BAJA and fabricate the buggy from scratch, starting from the design of the vehicle to fabrication of its various components from the roll cage, transmission, suspension, to the braking and steering system of the vehicle. They, then participate in the baja virtuals and on qualification, race their buggy in the Pithampur race track in Indore.
